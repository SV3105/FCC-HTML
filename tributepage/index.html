Dr.A.P.J Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man Of India

Here's a time line of Dr. Kalam's life:

  • 1931 - Born on October 15 in Rameshwaram ,Tamil Nadu.
  • 1954 - Graduated in Physics from Saint Joseph's College.
  • 1960 - Joined the DRDO as a scientist and later studied aerospace engineering at MIT.
  • 1969 - Transferred to ISRO and worked on rocket projects.
  • 1980-90 - Responsible for the development of AGNI and PRITHVI missiles.
  • 1992-99 - Served as the Chief Scientific Adviser to the PM and the Secretary of DRDO.
  • 1997 - Awarded Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honour.
  • 2002-07 - Served as the 11th President of India.
  • 2012 - Launched mission for the youth of the nation called “What Can I Give Movement”.
  • 2015 - Passed away on 27 July in Shillong, Meghalaya.

-By Abdul Kalam
